GEX DECODED PANE
Gamma Exposure Explained Simply — This Is the Hidden Force
GEX tells you what market makers (option dealers) MUST do mechanically as price moves. When GEX is POSITIVE (green bars on the chart), dealers are long gamma — they must BUY when price falls and SELL when price rises. This creates a rubber-band effect that pulls price BACK to the center. When GEX is NEGATIVE (red bars), dealers are short gamma — they SELL when price falls and BUY when price rises, amplifying moves in either direction.
